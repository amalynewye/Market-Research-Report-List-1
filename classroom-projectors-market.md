<p><p>Classroom projectors are categorized into vertical and horizontal types based on their installation and usage orientation. Vertical projectors are designed to be mounted on walls or ceilings, projecting images downward, making them suitable for large classrooms. In contrast, horizontal projectors are typically placed on desks or tables, projecting images directly in front of them, ideal for smaller settings. Both types enhance teaching experiences by facilitating dynamic visual presentations, engaging students, and improving information retention through visual aids.</p></p>
