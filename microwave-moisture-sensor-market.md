<p><p>The Microwave Moisture Sensor market comprises two primary types: Volumetric Moisture Sensors and Water Potential Moisture Sensors. Volumetric Moisture Sensors measure the water content within a material, such as soil or food, by analyzing the dielectric properties affected by moisture levels. In contrast, Water Potential Moisture Sensors assess the energy status of water in soil or plants, providing insights into plant stress and hydration. Both sensor types are essential for agricultural applications, food processing, and environmental monitoring, ensuring optimal moisture management.</p></p>
